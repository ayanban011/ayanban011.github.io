In my groundbreaking master thesis dissertation, I delved into the realm of document layout analysis, a crucial facet of modern information processing. Focusing on innovation and efficiency, I harnessed the power of cutting-edge Transformer-based semi-supervised learning techniques. By integrating Graph-Based Knowledge Distillation into this approach, I not only refined the understanding of document layouts but also paved the way for enhanced analysis accuracy. My research successfully demonstrated the potential of this amalgamation, providing a robust framework that pushes the boundaries of what's achievable in document layout analysis. This breakthrough is a testament to the convergence of AI methodologies, propelling the field forward and promising a future where document analysis is more insightful and precise than ever before.
